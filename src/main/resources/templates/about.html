ABOUT US

Established in May 2023, Edumission is a reputed and reliable man power solutions company in West Bengal. Edumission has been established with a whole and sole objective of providing end to end manpower recruitment solutions for both fresher and experienced candidates. Recently we are working all over the West Bengal with our office at Chakdaha, Nadia. We help candidate looking for jobs and companies looking for candidates at the same time, thus bridging the gap of job and man power near.

Edumission acts as a positive accelerator in progressing the man power recruitment process and man power organization to concentrate on your core business. We especially focus on individual needs and small and medium organization. Our end to end man power recruitment solution is designed to be cost effective.

MISSION

Edumission's mission is to become the provider of choice for manpower services by placing right candidates at the right company. We focus on to bring the best from every candidate. We aim at becoming a premier solution provider so that we can meet the needs of our candidates and clients from a single source to a complete turnkey solution to their Manpower needs with highest level of performance and quality.

VISION

Edumission's vision is wide and clear. Here we support companies and help candidate providing high standard man power services. We see us as a medium for making the world a better place to live in by helping candidates to get the right job and for companies to provide the right skilled candidates which will surely help in bringing peace and joy in the world. We offer different types of jobs like Back end Operation associate, Workshop manager, Service adviser, Operation manager, Engineers & Technician in different verticals for various companies. Edumission believes in keeping pace with the leading giants in the industry, taking every possible step, big and small alike.
